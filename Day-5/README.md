# DAY-5
Career Planning and Computer Hardware Lab Visit
## Date
07 July 2026
### Department
Computer Science & Engineering (CSE)
SL-4 Lab
### Objective
The objective of today's training session was to understand the various career opportunities available for a Computer Science & Engineering graduate in both jobs and startups. We also learned basic troubleshooting techniques for computer peripherals such as the keyboard and mouse. Later, we visited the Hardware Laboratory to observe computer hardware components in real life and understand their functions
### Topics Covered
#### 1. Career Opportunities for CSE Graduates
We discussed the different career paths available after completing a B.Tech in Computer Science & Engineering.
#### Categories Covered
##### Technical Career Options
- Software Engineer
- Full Stack Developer
- Front-End Developer
- Back-End Developer
- Mobile App Developer
- AI/ML Engineer
- Data Scientist
- Cybersecurity Analyst
- Cloud Engineer
##### Non-Technical Career Options
- Product Manager
- Project Manager
- Business Analyst
- Technical Recruiter
- IT Consultant
- Customer Success Manager
- Operations Manager
- Business Development Executive

#### 3. Choosing Personal Career Goals
Our training coordinator mam asked us to think about our future career and select our three favourite career options based on our interests, skills, and long-term goals. This activity encouraged us to plan our career path and understand the opportunities available in the IT industry.
#### 4. Keyboard and Mouse Troubleshooting
We learned some basic troubleshooting techniques for computer input devices.
##### Topics Covered
- Checking keyboard cable connections.
- Checking mouse cable and USB port connections.
- Cleaning the mouse sensor.
- Using a proper mouse pad for smooth tracking.
- Reconnecting or replacing faulty cables.
- Identifying hardware and software-related issues.
##### Learning Outcomes
- Understanding basic peripheral troubleshooting.
- Identifying common keyboard and mouse problems.
- Improving practical troubleshooting skills.
#### 5. Hardware Laboratory Visit

During the visit, we observed various computer hardware components and understood their functions.
##### Hardware Components Observed
- Motherboard
- Processor (CPU)
- RAM
- Hard Disk Drive (HDD)
- Solid State Drive (SSD)
- SMPS (Power Supply Unit)
- Cooling Fan & Heat Sink
- CMOS Battery
- Keyboard
- Mouse
- Monitor
- Computer Cabinet
##### Learning Outcomes
- Identifying computer hardware components.
- Understanding the function of each component.
- Observing real hardware instead of only studying theory.
- Improving practical knowledge of computer systems.
